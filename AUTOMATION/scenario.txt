1) Open this page https://czechibank.ostrava.digital in chrome browser,
2) Switch to dark mode,
3) Click Register button,
4) Generate RANDOM Full Name and fill it in Full name field
5) For this Full Name generate email and fill it in email field,
6) Generate random password at least 8 characters long and fill it in Password as well as Confirm password field,
7) Wait 2 seconds,
8) Click Register button,
9) Validate that registration was successfull and continue to the app. 
10) Validate that user's default account balance is 100000.0 Czechitokens.
11) Close the current browser tab.
12) If any error occurs during the process please close current active window.
